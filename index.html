Where Steaks Are Cooked To Perfection

A steakhouse is a restaurant focused on high-quality beef cuts (like ribeye, filet mignon, Tomahawk) cooked expertly, featuring an upscale ambiance, rich sides (creamed spinach, potatoes), extensive wine lists (especially reds), and attentive service, creating a luxurious dining experience often for special occasions, sometimes with classic dark wood decor, or a modern twist with broader menus.
